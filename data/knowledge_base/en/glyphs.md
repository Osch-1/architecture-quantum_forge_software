# The Glyphs

The **Glyphs** are the five simple battle-signs that every **ashwarden** learns to channel raw **Aether** through hand-gestures. Far cruder than the intricate spellcraft of the **veilweavers**, the Glyphs are fast, reliable, and usable in the middle of a fight — the difference between life and death on a hunt.

The five Glyphs are:

- **Ignar** — a cone of fire that burns and panics monsters; the warden's answer to packs of **mirewights** and **gravelurks**.
- **Vardh** — a blast of kinetic force that knocks foes back, breaks guards, and topples charging beasts.
- **Quel** — a protective ward that shields the caster from a blow or a monster's draining touch.
- **Yrn** — a glowing trap laid on the ground that holds and weakens what crosses it; essential against a **groveshade** or **sunwraith**.
- **Axin** — a sign that briefly sways a weak mind, calming a beast or bending a frightened man.

Mastery varies between wardens: **Derrin** is renowned for his Yrn traps, while **Varmun** leans hard on aggressive Ignar. The Glyphs draw on the same **Aether** unleashed by the **Riftfall**, but a warden's capacity is limited — overreach leaves them drained and vulnerable, so the signs are used to shape a fight, not to replace the **moonsteel** blade.
