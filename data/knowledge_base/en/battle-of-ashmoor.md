# The Battle of Ashmoor

The **Battle of Ashmoor** was the great clash that halted — for a time — the northward advance of the **Ulreth Imperium**. On the bleak hill of Ashmoor, a hastily assembled alliance of northern kingdoms, led in part by **Veldoria**, met the imperial army in a battle that would decide the fate of the continent.

The fighting was savage and the losses staggering on both sides. The northern host held the hill, and many credit the **veilweavers** of the **Conclave of Veils** — among them **Veymira of Halgar** — whose Aetheric fire broke the imperial charge at a fearful cost in mages' lives. The victory bought the north years of uneasy independence, but it did not end Emperor **Azmar var Ulreth's** ambitions.

Ashmoor left scars that outlasted the battle. So many dead lay unburied that the hill became a notorious nest of **gravelurks** and a haunt of **sunwraiths**, drawing ashwardens for years afterward. The bard **Lirio** made the battle famous in verse, and to this day "the cost of Ashmoor" is a saying in **Aerdmir** for a victory that wins everything except peace.
