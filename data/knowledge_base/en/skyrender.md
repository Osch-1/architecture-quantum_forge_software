# Skyrender

The **skyrender** is a great winged predator of **Aerdmir**, a hybrid beast with the foreparts of a mountain cat and the wings and talons of an eagle. Skyrenders nest on the storm-battered cliffs of **Stormholt** and along the high ridges above **Mokrad**, where they hunt cattle, horses, and the occasional careless traveller. A grown skyrender can carry off a full-grown ox, and its dive is announced only by a sudden shadow and the shriek of torn air.

Hunting a skyrender is classic **ashwarden** work. Because the beast is part magical in origin — a leftover of the **Riftfall** that once seeded **Aerdmir** with creatures from other spheres — ordinary steel barely marks it; a blade of **moonsteel** is required. Wardens of the **Lodge of the Lynx** recommend grounding the creature first with the **Vardh** glyph, raising the **Quel** glyph against its diving strikes, and finishing it once it is forced to fight on foot. The brave drink **Stormdraught** beforehand to survive the first blow.

Skyrender feathers, talons, and especially the bezoar from its gut are prized by **veilweavers** of the **Conclave of Veils**, who use them in wards against airborne threats. More than one contract posted in **Brennar** has set a hunter against a skyrender that had grown bold enough to snatch children from the fields — and more than one inexperienced **ashwarden** has died learning that the beast is far more agile in the air than any creature its size has a right to be.
