# Gravelurk

The **gravelurk** is a corpse-eating monster that infests graveyards, battlefields, and the tunnels beneath old cities across **Aerdmir**. Lean, grey, and unnervingly fast, gravelurks hunt in packs, dragging down the wounded and feasting on the dead. Where many have died and gone unburied, their numbers can swell into a true plague.

Though individually weak, a gravelurk pack is deadly through sheer numbers and ferocity, and a frenzied one ignores wounds that would fell a beast twice its size. **Ashwardens** clear them with the **Vardh** glyph to scatter the pack, the **Ignar** glyph to burn the swarm, and a **moonsteel** blade for the survivors; a dose of **Stormdraught** helps a lone hunter weather the rush. **Varmun** is said to take gravelurk contracts no one else will touch.

After great battles — such as the slaughter at the **Battle of Ashmoor** — gravelurks gather in such numbers that whole regions become impassable. The sewers of **Brennar** and the crypts beneath **Drennan** are notorious nests, and clearing them is steady, grim, and thankless work for any ashwarden short of coin.
