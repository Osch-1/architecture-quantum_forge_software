# The Dolgan

The **Dolgan** are a short, broad, and famously stubborn folk of **Aerdmir**, renowned as miners, smiths, and engineers. Dwelling in mountain holds and the hill-country between human kingdoms, they forged their reputation on craftsmanship no human smith can match — and it is Dolgan metallurgy that underlies the rites used to work **moonsteel** for the ashwardens of **Vael Korhal**.

Though grudgingly tolerated for their skills, the Dolgan suffer much of the same prejudice as the **Sylvani**. Pushed out of the best trades and barred from many guilds, they have grown shrewd, clannish, and slow to trust outsiders. Many run banks, foundries, and workshops in cities like **Brennar**, where their wealth buys a measure of safety that their birth does not.

Unlike the **Sylvani**, the Dolgan rarely dream of reclaiming a lost golden age; they are pragmatists who endure, adapt, and profit. Yet shared mistreatment means Dolgan coin and Dolgan steel sometimes find their way to the **Thornblades**, and a Dolgan who has been wronged is an enemy with a very long memory — and very deep pockets.
