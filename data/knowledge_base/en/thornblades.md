# Thornblades

The **Thornblades** are bands of **Sylvani** guerrilla fighters waging a desperate war against the human kingdoms of **Aerdmir** that have driven their people from their ancestral forests. Taking their name from the thorned arrows they loose from cover, they strike at patrols, supply lines, and frontier settlements, then vanish into the woods before any army can answer.

To the humans of **Veldoria** and the **Ulreth Imperium**, the Thornblades are murderers and bandits; to the oppressed **Sylvani** and many **Dolgan**, they are freedom fighters and the last defenders of a vanishing world. The truth is grimmer than either tale: years of reprisal and massacre have hardened the Thornblades into something merciless, and innocents on both sides pay the price.

The Thornblades shelter in deep forests — sometimes near the groves of a **groveshade**, which they will not disturb — and in the slums of cities like **Brennar** and **Drennan**, where displaced Sylvani hide. Powers such as the Imperium and the **Conclave of Veils** are not above using them as pawns. Ashwardens like **Korven of Drael** try to stay clear of the conflict, but on the road neutrality is a luxury few are allowed to keep.
