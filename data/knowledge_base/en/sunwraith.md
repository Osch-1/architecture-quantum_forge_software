# Sunwraith

The **sunwraith** is a vengeful spirit that haunts the open fields of **Aerdmir** under the blazing midday sun, born from a woman who died violently and unjustly before her time. Appearing as a shimmering, blindingly bright figure, it strikes travellers and field-workers with heat, madness, and a touch that drains the life from the body.

Unlike beasts of flesh, a sunwraith cannot simply be cut down with steel alone. The **Lodge of the Lynx** teaches that the creature must first be pinned with the **Yrn** glyph, which anchors it to the mortal world, and only then struck with a **moonsteel** blade. A draught of **Owldraught** sharpens a hunter's sight against the wraith's searing glare, and the **Quel** glyph blunts its withering touch.

Sunwraiths are most common in the war-torn fields of **Mokrad** and **Veldoria**, where countless deaths have soaked the earth. Folk wisdom holds that a sunwraith can sometimes be laid to rest by righting the wrong that created it — returning stolen bones, punishing a killer — but few have the time or courage to try, and most simply send for an **ashwarden** like **Korven of Drael**.
