# Brisa Aldwen

**Brisa Aldwen** is a young, warm-hearted **veilweaver** and a member of the **Conclave of Veils**. Famous for her copper-red hair and her talent with healing and fire, she is far kinder than most who master the **Aether**, which both endears her to allies and makes her vulnerable in the Conclave's ruthless politics.

Brisa is a long-time friend and occasional advisor to the **ashwarden Korven of Drael**, and she shares with **Veymira of Halgar** a complicated history of rivalry and reluctant trust. When the cities of **Brennar** turned against Aether-users during a wave of witch-hunts, Brisa risked her life smuggling fellow veilweavers out of the city, earning both gratitude and a price on her head.

Unlike the colder members of the Conclave, Brisa believes the order should protect ordinary folk rather than rule them. She has studied the strange surges of Aether that followed the **Riftfall**, and she was among the first to recognise that the **Riven Blood** carried by **Aysa** was no mere legend. Her counsel often steers Korven toward mercy where another would draw moonsteel.
