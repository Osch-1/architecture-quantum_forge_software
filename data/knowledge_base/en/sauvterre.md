# Sauvterre

**Sauvterre** is a small, sun-drenched duchy in the south of **Aerdmir**, famed for its vineyards, its tournaments, and its stubborn devotion to the ideals of knightly chivalry. Spared the worst of the continent's wars, it has become a fairy-tale land of painted castles and grand festivals — a glittering surface beneath which older, darker things still stir.

For an **ashwarden**, Sauvterre is deceptively dangerous work. Its romantic legends draw rare and powerful monsters, and its nobles prefer their contracts handled discreetly, lest a hunt spoil the illusion of a perfect realm. **Korven of Drael** once spent a long season here untangling a curse that bred a **curseborn** beneath a noble house — a tale the bard **Lirio** still sings.

The duchy guards its autonomy carefully, paying tribute to the **Ulreth Imperium** while keeping its own knights and customs. Its vineyards make it wealthy, its isolation makes it proud, and its love of beautiful stories makes it slow to admit when something truly monstrous has taken root behind the tournament fields.
