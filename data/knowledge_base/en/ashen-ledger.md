# The Ashen Ledger

The **Ashen Ledger** is the great record-book of the **Lodge of the Lynx**, kept in
the vaults of the mountain fortress **Vael Korhal**. For centuries the **ashwardens**
have written into it the names of their dead, the contracts they swore, and the
beasts they slew, so that no oath and no sacrifice would be forgotten.

Only the lodge's last mentor, old **Olvar**, keeps the key to the vault where the
Ledger rests. An apprentice may read it only after passing the **Trial of Ashes**,
and even then under his watchful eye. When a warden dies on a contract, his
**warden's sigil** is laid upon the page that bears his name, and the Ledger is
closed for a day of mourning.
