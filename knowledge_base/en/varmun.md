# Varmun

**Varmun** is an **ashwarden** of the **Lodge of the Lynx**, the youngest of the wardens trained at **Vael Korhal** alongside **Korven of Drael** and **Derrin**. Sharp-tongued, cynical, and quick to anger, Varmun wears his bitterness openly: he resents a world that takes the ashwardens' coin and spits on their kind, and he says so loudly in every tavern from **Mokrad** to **Stormholt**.

For all his sourness, Varmun is a formidable hunter, favouring aggressive use of the **Ignar** glyph and a reckless, blade-first style that has nearly killed him more than once. He is fastest of the three friends and the most willing to take a desperate contract for desperate pay.

Old **Olvar** despairs of Varmun's temper but trusts his skill, and Korven counts him a brother despite their constant bickering. Beneath the armour of complaint, Varmun keeps a private code: he will not let a child go unprotected, and he has never once abandoned a fellow warden on a hunt — not against a **gravelurk**, not against the riders of the **Wraith Host**.
