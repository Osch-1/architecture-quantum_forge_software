# Brennar

**Brennar** is the largest free city of **Aerdmir** — a sprawling, wealthy port that answers to no crown and worships coin above all. Its crowded docks, banking houses, and printing presses make it the beating heart of trade on the continent, and its temples and crime syndicates wrestle endlessly for control of its streets.

Brennar is a dangerous place for users of the **Aether**: waves of witch-hunting have seen **veilweavers** burned or driven underground, and it was here that **Brisa Aldwen** risked everything to smuggle her fellows to safety. For an **ashwarden** like **Korven of Drael**, the city offers steady work — its sewers and canals breed **mirewights** and **gravelurks** — but little welcome.

The city is a web of factions: merchant princes, a powerful temple guard, smuggler gangs, and cells of the **Thornblades** hiding among the oppressed **Sylvani**. The bard **Lirio** loves Brennar for its taverns and audiences, even after a stay in one of its cells. Whoever controls Brennar's gold controls much of **Aerdmir** — a fact the **Ulreth Imperium** understands all too well.
