# Conclave of Veils

The **Conclave of Veils** is the great assembly of **veilweavers** — the masters of the **Aether** — and one of the most powerful and secretive institutions in **Aerdmir**. Officially a council that trains mages and advises rulers, the Conclave is in truth a web of ambition, rivalry, and hidden agendas that quietly shapes the fate of kingdoms.

Its members serve as court advisors, healers, and spymasters from **Veldoria** to the **Ulreth Imperium**, and few thrones make a decision without a veilweaver whispering nearby. Yet the Conclave guards its independence jealously, and Emperor **Azmar var Ulreth's** efforts to bring it to heel have bred deep and dangerous factions within it. Among its notable members are the coldly brilliant **Veymira of Halgar** and the kinder **Brisa Aldwen**, who often stand on opposite sides of its debates.

The Conclave's greatest obsession is the legacy of the **Riftfall** and the **Riven Blood** carried by the girl **Aysa**, which some members would protect and others would seize. Its internal feuds erupted catastrophically in the **Spire Betrayal**, an event that shattered old alliances and set veilweaver against veilweaver across the continent.
