# Azmar var Ulreth

**Azmar var Ulreth** is the reigning emperor of the **Ulreth Imperium**, the vast southern power whose black-and-gold banners have swallowed kingdom after kingdom across **Aerdmir**. Cold, brilliant, and patient, Azmar rules through a disciplined bureaucracy and a fearsome army, and he is feared even by the **veilweavers** of the **Conclave of Veils**, whom he keeps on a short leash.

For all his conquests, Azmar's deepest interest is the girl **Aysa** and the **Riven Blood** in her veins, which old imperial prophecies tie to dominion over the rifts left by the **Riftfall**. His agents have hunted her across the continent, bringing him into quiet, dangerous opposition with the **ashwarden Korven of Drael** and the **veilweaver Veymira of Halgar**.

Azmar is no simple tyrant: he is capable of restraint, even honour, when it serves his designs, and some of his reforms have brought order to lands long torn by war. But his ambition is boundless, and those who mistake his calm for kindness rarely survive the lesson. His role in the **Spire Betrayal** remains a matter of bitter dispute.
