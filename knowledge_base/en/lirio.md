# Lirio

**Lirio** is a celebrated travelling bard and the most loyal — and most exasperating — companion of the **ashwarden Korven of Drael**. Quick of wit and quicker of tongue, he wanders the taverns and courts of **Aerdmir** trading songs for coin, wine, and the company of admirers, while quietly turning Korven's grim contracts into ballads that have made the ashwarden famous from **Brennar** to **Sauvterre**.

Though no fighter, Lirio has a gift for talking his way into (and out of) trouble, and his songs carry more truth than the powerful would like. It was Lirio who first spread word of the **Battle of Ashmoor** in verse, and Lirio whose mocking couplets about the **Ulreth Imperium** earned him a spell in a Drennan dungeon.

Beneath the vanity, Lirio is braver than he admits: he has stood beside Korven against a **groveshade**, carried messages for **Brisa Aldwen** through hostile streets, and never once betrayed a friend. His chronicles are, for many common folk, the only history of the ashwardens they will ever hear.
