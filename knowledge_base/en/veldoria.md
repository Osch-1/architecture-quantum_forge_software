# Veldoria

**Veldoria** is one of the old northern kingdoms of **Aerdmir**, a land of fertile river valleys, walled towns, and proud, quarrelsome nobles. Long a regional power, Veldoria has been ground down by decades of war with the expanding **Ulreth Imperium**, and much of its countryside — especially toward the marshes of **Mokrad** — lies ravaged and half-abandoned.

The Veldorian crown has a complicated relationship with the **ashwardens**: its kings hire them when **mirewights** rise from the rivers or a **sunwraith** haunts the harvest fields, yet its priests preach against the mutants as unnatural. **Korven of Drael** has taken many contracts here, and more than once been cheated of his pay.

Veldoria is also a centre of intrigue. Its court hosts agents of the **Conclave of Veils**, including at times **Veymira of Halgar**, and its faltering independence makes it a key prize in Emperor **Azmar var Ulreth's** designs. Should Veldoria fall, little would stand between the Imperium and the north — a fear that shadows every treaty signed in its halls.
