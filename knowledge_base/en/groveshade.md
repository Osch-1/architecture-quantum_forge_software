# Groveshade

The **groveshade** is an ancient forest spirit of **Aerdmir**, a towering figure of living wood, antlers, and tangled roots that embodies the will of the deep woods. Rarely seen and rarely provoked without cause, a groveshade will defend its grove with terrible fury, commanding crows, wolves, and the very trees against intruders.

Groveshades are among the most dangerous contracts an **ashwarden** can take. They cannot be poisoned, tire, or be reasoned with, and they regenerate unless burned. The standard tactic taught at the **Lodge of the Lynx** is to lure the creature into prepared ground, bind it with the **Yrn** glyph, and destroy it with fire from the **Ignar** glyph and a blade of **moonsteel**. **Derrin** is said to be a master of trapping them; **Korven of Drael** prefers to talk villagers out of the hunt entirely, for a groveshade left in peace harms no one.

In the marsh-forests of **Mokrad**, some communities still leave offerings to their local groveshade rather than risk its wrath. The **Sylvani** revere these spirits as guardians of the old world, and they consider their slaughter by human hunters a grievous crime.
