# Ulreth Imperium

The **Ulreth Imperium** is the dominant power of southern **Aerdmir** and the great conqueror of the age. Under its black-and-gold banners, a disciplined army and an efficient bureaucracy have absorbed dozens of kingdoms, and its frontier now presses hard against **Veldoria** and the northern lands.

Ruled by Emperor **Azmar var Ulreth**, the Imperium prizes order, law, and loyalty above tradition. It tolerates **veilweavers** only under strict imperial control, keeping the **Conclave of Veils** at arm's length, and it regards the independent **ashwardens** with suspicion. Imperial agents range far beyond its borders, hunting for the girl **Aysa** and the **Riven Blood** that old prophecies link to mastery of the rifts left by the **Riftfall**.

To its subjects the Imperium can bring genuine peace and prosperity after the chaos of petty wars; to its enemies it is a remorseless machine of conquest. Its standing army crushed an allied host at the **Battle of Ashmoor**, and only stubborn resistance, harsh terrain, and the intrigues of the **Spire Betrayal** have kept the whole continent from falling under Azmar's crown.
